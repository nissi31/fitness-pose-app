# 🤖 AI Fitness Coach (Gradio Space)

Upload your fitness exercise video (Back Squat, Barbell Row, Overhead Press, or Jumping Jack) and get real-time form feedback.

- Select your exercise
- Upload your video (.mp4 preferred)
- See your feedback and annotated video

**Model:** YOLOv8 pose estimation  
**Framework:** Gradio

> Note: Feedback is shown as time-marked text. For best results, use short videos of less than 5 seconds with clear, unobstructed views.
